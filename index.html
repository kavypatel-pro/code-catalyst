About PM Internship Scheme

Empowering India's youth through technology and opportunity

Our Mission

The PM Internship Scheme is a flagship initiative by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India, designed to bridge the gap between academic learning and practical industry experience. Our AI-powered recommendation engine ensures that every student, regardless of their background or location, can find the perfect internship opportunity.

Why We Built This

With hundreds of internship opportunities available across India, students often struggle to find the right match for their skills and interests. Our intelligent system analyzes student profiles, academic backgrounds, and career aspirations to provide personalized recommendations, making the internship search process simple and effective.

Our Commitment

We are committed to making quality internship opportunities accessible to students from rural areas, tribal districts, urban slums, and remote colleges. Our platform is designed with simplicity in mind, ensuring that first-generation learners and students with limited digital exposure can easily navigate and benefit from our services.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI recommendation system work?

Our AI analyzes your profile, education, skills, interests, and location preferences to match you with the most suitable internship opportunities from our database.

Is registration free?

Yes, registration and using our platform is completely free for all students across India.

Can I apply for multiple internships?

Absolutely! You can apply for as many internships as you want. We recommend focusing on the ones that best match your profile.

What if I'm from a rural area with limited internet?

Our platform is optimized for low-bandwidth connections and works well on basic smartphones. We also provide offline support through our helpline.
